Born as a United States citizen on August 13th, 1992 in Washington DC, Akif Hamdy Eisa is of mixed Albanian and Egyptian descent. His father, Hamdy Mahmoud Eisa was an Albanian-Egyptian geneticist who came from Egypt to the United States of America in the 1960s and his mother was a United Nations refugee of 100% Albanian descent. He is The youngest of 5 siblings who share the same geneticist father.
